The Clear30 4/21 Weed Break

The 4/21 Weed Break is a chance to do some spring cleaning of your brain to clear some of the smoke from 4/20 and reset your endocannabinoid system from regular use for 30 days. When we do something regularly, we start to forget what life is like without it. We start to get used to the new (or not-so-new) normal.

Who is it for?

The 4/21 Weed Break is for anyone who has said to themselves at some point, “maybe I should take a break.” It is for anyone whose friends or loved ones have said, “maybe you should take a break.” There’s no better time than after the festivities of 4/20 to dive into an exploration of a month of resetting.
