How Clear30 works

Three steps to a clearer mind

Set your intention

Choose your goal: quit, cut back, or reset your relationship with weed.

Build your plan

Get a personalized path with daily check-ins, progress tracking, and support tools.

Track & grow

Log daily, spot patterns, and build momentum one day at a time.

1. Set intention You define the goal. Clear30 asks what you want to change, why it matters, and builds a personalized program around your answer.

2. Track daily Quick daily check-ins, streaks, craving logs, and money saved. Stay honest with yourself without it feeling like a chore.

3. See Progress Watch your brain, lungs, and heart recover in real time. Unlock milestones and achievements as you go.

4. Learn & Reflect Daily lessons, meditations, journaling, and videos that help you understand your habits and actually change them.

5. Get support AI chat, accountability buddies, peer groups, and access to Dr. Fred. You're never doing this alone.

6. Sustain Progress Clear30 keeps working after day 30. Community, ongoing lessons, and feedback tools that make this a lifestyle, not just a challenge.
