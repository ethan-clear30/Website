How the Pilot Works

The pilot runs for 60 days at no cost to your institution. We handle the technology, student support, and reporting. Your team handles distribution through channels you already use. Your team gets a real-time campus portal to track student engagement throughout, and at the end we review results together against the goals you set at kickoff.

The Partnership

What Clear30 Provides

Program Access
  • University-specific activation link and QR code
  • Real-time campus portal to track student engagement
  • In-app support for students throughout the pilot
Distribution
  • Full distribution kit with ready-to-use copy, creative assets, and channel guidance
Reporting
  • Mid-pilot engagement snapshot (shareable one-pager)
  • Pilot Summary Report formatted for internal budget conversations

What We Ask of Your Team

People
  • A Campus Champion who owns distribution day-to-day
  • A Senior Contact who helps define success criteria
Distribution
  • At least 2 high-impact channels (e.g., LMS banner, schoolwide email, official social post)
  • One supplementary channel (e.g., newsletter, student org partnership)
Participation
  • Attendance at kickoff, mid-pilot check-in, and Partnership Review
